Aufgaben

Becoming part of Mercedes-Benz means finding your individual role and workspace to unleash your talents to the fullest. It means becoming your best self in a global automotive company striving towards sustainable luxury. Empowered by visionary colleagues who share the same pioneering spirit.

Together for excellence.

Mercedes-Benz Mobility at a Glance
With around 10,000 employees, Mercedes-Benz Mobility AG specialises in financial and mobility services. The products range from financing, leasing, vehicle subscriptions, rental and fleet management to digital services for charging and payments, as well as insurance and innovative mobility services.
Mercedes-Benz Mobility is a division of Mercedes-Benz Group AG, which also includes the vehicle manufacturer Mercedes-Benz AG, one of the largest suppliers of premium and luxury passenger cars and vans. As a financial services provider, Mercedes-Benz Mobility supports sales of the Mercedes-Benz Group's automotive brands worldwide. In Germany, Mercedes-Benz Bank AG, as part of Mercedes-Benz Mobility AG is one of the leading vehicle financing banks and offers around one million customers financial flexibility as well as deposit business and fixed-interest-rate accounts in the direct banking business.
Flexible mobility offers such as Mercedes-Benz Rent or EQ subscriptions, enable an uncomplicated and flexible entry into e-mobility, complementing the service portfolio. With its subsidiary Athlon, Mercedes-Benz Mobility is present in Europe in operational fleet management and vehicle leasing and is responsible for around 400,000 vehicles.
In addition, Mercedes-Benz Mobility investments in the market for urban mobility services: The joint ventures FREE NOW, SHARE NOW and CHARGE NOW combine solutions for ride-hailing and multi-modal mobility, car sharing and electric vehicle charging. At the same time, the portfolio also includes StarRides – a limousine transport service joint venture in China – and the participation in the Berlin chauffeur service Blacklane.

Mercedes-Benz Mobility lives diversity and inclusion
Diversity and inclusion are not only a concern of ours, we actively shape them. Employees from 100 different nations enrich our culture. Through numerous initiatives, we promote intercultural understanding, respect for one another, openness and willingness to change within the company, as well as non-profit organizations that are committed to this.

Data, Analytics & BI
The Data, Analytics & Bi department at Mercedes-Benz Mobility is operating and further developing our cloud-based Data Lake & Advanced Analytics platform. By onboarding further global markets and offering E2E Advanced Analytics products – hand in hand with providing guidance on the compliance and information security side – we are one of the main drivers of the company-wide digital transformation. Within this framework, we play a role of great importance for numerous cross-organizational projects that have focused on digitizing their business processes and/or setting up fully digitized business models.

Our Team Advanced Analytics & Data-Driven Solutions
From Stuttgart, we join forces with Mercedes-Benz Mobility markets and departments to bring the benefits of Advanced Analytics solutions to our organization. With a team of data scientists, IT experts and project managers, we develop and implement data-driven solutions to support our business. Furthermore, we are driving the strategy program data-driven company within MBM 2025 and aim to transform our company to a data-centric organization that embraces the value of data and knows how to make use of it.

Your tasks will be:

  • As a Junior Data Scientist you will be supporting the implementation of Artificial Intelligence use cases with a broad range of variety
  • You will implement Advanced Analytics workflows in an end2end fashion: Data preparation, feature engineering, feature selection, predictive modelling, validation and application of the algorithm on productive data
  • You will deploy and integrate your workflows in our Advanced Analytics IT infrastructure Spire (Microsoft Azure Cloud) for operations and therefore closely collaborate with our Cloud team (Spire-team) and IT
  • You will be part of an agile data science scrum team and develop your workflows in an interactive fashion based on sprints
  • You will collaborate continuously with the senior data scientists of the team and subject matter experts from the business units
  • You will support the team and respective business and IT responsibles in our MBM markets to implement our AI products and develop tailor-sized approaches for specific market needs
  • As a Junior Data Scientist you will work closely together with the senior data scientist staff as well as the product owners and gradually take over more responsibility for the development of high quality AI


Above, we offer you a well-structured educational plan that focuses on your individual needs as a junior data scientist to driver your career within data science and foster your development on each step of the way.

Qualifikationen

What you bring to the table:

  • Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics or related field
  • Outstanding knowledge in mathematics and statistics
  • High motivation for developing and implementing data-driven projects
  • Solid understanding of data science: Machine learning, predictive modelling, and A/B Testing
  • Good knowledge in Data Analytics programming e.g. Python, SQL, R, Spark
  • Interest and motivation to get to know and work with Microsoft Azure Services e.g. DataLake, DataFactory, Databricks, and MLflow
  • Knowledge of Mercedes-Benz Mobility and its business model
  • Very good English language skills
  • German language skills would be a plus
  • Experience with cloud technology is a plus



Personal skills


  • Teamplayer with excellent interpersonal skills to interact with multiple stakeholders
  • Very good communication skills to work with business customers as well as technical stakeholders
  • Excellent analytical and conceptual skills
  • Strong motivation and enthusiasm to work with a customer-centric mindset
  • Flexibility and adaptability to work in a dynamic international and interdisciplinary team


Additional information:

It is a permanent position.

It doesn’t work completely without formalities. When sending your online application, please attach your CV, an individual letter and any references you may have (max. 5 MB) and mark your application documents as "relevant for this application" in the online form.

We particularly welcome online applications from candidates with disabilities or similar impairments in direct response to this job advertisement. If you have any questions, you can contact the local disability officer once you have submitted your application form, who will gladly assist you in the onward application process: sbv@mercedes-benz.com

If you have any questions regarding the application process, please contact HR Services by e-mail at hrservices@mercedes-benz.com or the chatbot on our career page via the plus symbol.

Please understand that we no longer accept paper applications and that there is no right to get your documents returned.
